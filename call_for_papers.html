Call for Papers

We invite high-quality submissions addressing integrated ego-exo sensing for smart mobility. Relevant topics include, but are not limited to:

  • Collection of synchronized egocentric and exocentric data for smart mobility
  • Detection and generation of safety-critical and edge-case scenarios via ego-exo sensing
  • Efficient annotation of egocentric and exocentric data
  • Privacy-preserving and anonymization techniques for cross-sensor data
  • Multimodal large models for scene understanding, cooperative driving, and multi-agent decision-making
  • Modeling and simulation of heterogeneous traffic behavior via integrated ego-exo sensing modalities
  • Data generation for training and testing cooperative driving systems
  • Modeling of multi-agent interaction and decision processes
  • Benchmarking safety, pollution, and efficiency in smart mobility systems
